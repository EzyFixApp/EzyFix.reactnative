# Privacy Policy - EzyFix

**Effective Date:** November 22, 2025

## Information We Collect

We collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
- Location data:
  - GPS for technician matching and routing
  - **Background location tracking**: When you have an active order (as a technician), we track your location in the background to show real-time location to customers. You can disable this but tracking won't work.
- Device information (OS, model, IP, device ID)
- Usage data (app interactions, crash logs, session data)
- **Local storage**: App settings and preferences stored on your device (not shared with servers)
- **Microphone**: Used when you send voice messages or make support calls (only when you actively use it)
- **Exact alarms**: Permission to schedule precise appointment reminders

## How We Use Information

- Connect customers with technicians
- Process bookings and payments
- Send order notifications
- Improve app experience
- Detect fraud and abuse

## Information Sharing

We share data with:
- Technicians (customer contact info for orders)
- Payment processors (PayOS)
- Service providers (Google Maps, Firebase)
- Legal authorities (when required)

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

## Your Rights

- Access and edit your information
- Delete your account (Settings > Delete Account)
- Opt-out of marketing notifications
- Turn off background location: Settings > Permissions > Location > "Only while using the app"
- Turn off microphone: Device Settings > App permissions > Microphone
- Download your data

## Data Security

- SSL/TLS encryption
- Password hashing (bcrypt)
- Two-factor authentication
- Regular security audits

Email: privacy@ezyfix.site
